### 1. What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening? 
* Be focused and avoid daydream.
* Listen carefully to what other person is saying.
* Don't interrupt the person during the conversion.
* Show you interest.
* Reply them with relevant and meaningful answers.
* Make sure to take notes of the important points.

### 2. According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?
* Listen carefully without interrupting.
* Understand the meaning behind what the other person is saying
* Reflect back whatever you understood.
* Also check if you understood it correctly.
* Acknowledge their feelings.
* Respond only after understanding fully.

### 3. What are the obstacles in your listening process?
* I pay attention only if the conversation is interesting.
* I Get distracted by small things.
* I interrupt people if I think I already understand what they are saying.
* Sometimes I am physically present but mentally not.

### 4. What can you do to improve your listening?
* I will try to stay focused and avoid distractions.
* I will listen completely before responding.
* I will make sure I am mentally present and not just physically.
* I will show some interest in the conversation.
